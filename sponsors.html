Our Sponsors

We thank all of our future and current sponsors that have helped us with our mission to promote STEM.

If you would like to sponsor the Circuit Breakers, please email the team's mentor Bhaskar Marthi [bmarthi@gmail.com]!

REV Robotics helps us with robot parts and is a huge part of the FTC community, providing parts made specifically for FIRST Robotics.

The Michigan Department of Education is a big help to us and especially smaller rookie teams that need a hand. They give us and others a good amount of money and we are proud to be working with them.

Arconic sponsored us this year and we are proud to have been sponsored.

FCA Foundation helps sponsor FTC robotics teams.

Why sponsor us?

● There are 800-1000 students and parents at an average FTC competition, and we’ll be attending at least 3 this year.
● If you sponsor us, up to 3000 people will gain knowledge about your business and its mission.

Tiers

● Bronze Sponsors ($100)- The sponsor’s name and a brief description will be on the team website.
● Silver Sponsors ($250)- The sponsor’s name and logo will be on Team T-Shirts.
● Gold Sponsors ($500)- The sponsor’s name and logo will be on Team T-Shirts and Banners.
● Platinum Sponsors ($1000)- The sponsor’s name and logo will be on the robot.
● Legendary Sponsors ($5000)- The sponsor’s name and logo will be PROMINENTLY displayed at Team workspaces.

Previous Sponsors

Buffalo Wild Wings sponsored us previously with a few fundraising events and some money. They also requested a team dinner which was great. We liked working with them.

SECO was one of our favorite sponsors. They gave us a tour and a pizza party and we showed them our robot. It was a nice experience and look forward to working with them again.
